This project was created for the course Art and Artificial Intelligence in Aalto University fall 2020.

It utilizes face-api.js to detect face and expressions from live video, and then draws corresponding emojis over the detected faces.

Code inspired from https://github.com/WebDevSimplified/Face-Detection-JavaScript.

Source available on GitHub.


Some food for thought and expirements:

  • What happens when there are multiple faces in the video?
  • Can you use a picture of face? Try a printed face or an image from your mobile phone.
  • How about a face mask?
